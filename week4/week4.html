Project 4: 3D Modeling and Printing

Container (with a lid!)

This week, I was tasked with using my knowledge of digital fabrication and 3D printing to design and print a 3D object that I couldn't cut on the laser cutter. I decided that I wanted to make some sort of mug with a screwable cap



I designed the container to have a little rounded handle, a cap, and a threaded exterior so that the cap could screw on. However, when I printed out the cap and the container, I realized that the cap was too small for the container and couldn't actually close it!




So, back to the drawing board! I redesigned the cup so that the threads would be sized perfectly and so that the cap would actually fit on top of the container. This time it worked, and I still use the cup to water my cactus today!
